DB Schenker and the Commonwealth Games

The Commonwealth Games is an event with a rich history of sporting excellence and fierce competition. Schenker was appointed to tackle a multitude of challenging freight, customs, and logistics tasks for the games and the accompanying Melburne Cultural Festival.

DB Schenker and the Volvo Ocean Race

The Volvo Ocean Race - a grueling 7-month, 31,000-nautical mile journey over the world's oceans. As the official logistics supplier for the race, Schenker Global Sports Events provides global logistics solutions and services.

DB Schenker Global Sports Events

Sports events call for precision, preparation, performance, and timing. We deliver world-class, comprehensive sports events services worldwide.
