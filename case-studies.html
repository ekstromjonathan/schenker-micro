Case Studies

Service for H&M in Bulgaria

Hennes & Mauritz (H&M), one of the world’s leading fashion companies, which over the last two years has been expanding in Bulgaria, recently placed an order with the Bulgarian national organization Schenker EOOD and a team of around 50 employees focussing on logistical storage and transport.

Cutting-edge laundry facility for Sochi

On behalf of Herbert Kannegiesser GmbH – a global leader in laundry technology – DB Schenker moved a cutting-edge laundry facility from Minden, Germany to the Russian Black Sea city of Sochi. The facility, valued at approximately ten million euros, will be used during the Winter Olympics in February 2014.
